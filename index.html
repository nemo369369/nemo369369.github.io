About Vanguard IT Consulting

Vanguard IT Consulting LLC is an innovative IT service provider based in Apopka, Florida, serving small businesses throughout the Orlando area. We specialize in delivering enterprise-grade IT solutions at prices small businesses can afford.

Our unique approach leverages cutting-edge AI technology to automate routine IT tasks, provide proactive maintenance, and deliver exceptional service with minimal human intervention. This allows us to offer premium IT support at a fraction of traditional costs.

Founded with a mission to democratize access to quality IT services, we're committed to helping small businesses compete with larger enterprises by providing the technological foundation they need to succeed.
